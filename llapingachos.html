Llapingachos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup of cheese (queso fresco or mozzarella), grated
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon of achiote powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes until they are soft. Drain and mash them in a bowl.
  2. Add the grated cheese, chopped onion, achiote powder, and salt to the mashed potatoes. Mix well until everything is combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into flat discs.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the llapingachos until they are golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with aji sauce and avocado slices.
