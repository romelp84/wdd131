Fritada Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs (900 g) pork belly or pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons achiote paste
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups orange juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine pork, water, achiote paste, minced garlic, chopped onion, and chopped tomatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until pork is tender, about 1.5 to 2 hours.
  2. Once the pork is tender, remove it from the pot and shred it using two forks.
  3. In a skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the shredded pork and fry until it turns crispy.
  4. Transfer the crispy pork to a serving platter and drizzle with orange juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot with mote (hominy), llapingachos (potato cakes), and avocado slices.
