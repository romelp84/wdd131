Encebollado Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (5 oz) tuna in water, drained
  • 4 cups fish or chicken broth
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (aji criollo)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring fish or chicken broth to a boil. Add sliced red onions and cook until softened.
  2. Add diced tomatoes and chopped cilantro to the pot. Stir in ketchup, lime juice, and hot sauce.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Add drained tuna to the pot and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with boiled yuca or plantain chips.
