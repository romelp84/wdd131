Site Plan - Ecuadorian Food Recipes

Site Name

Site Name: Ecuadorian Recipes Delight

Reason for Selection: The name reflects the focus on delightful and authentic Ecuadorian recipes, appealing to both locals and international cuisine enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a comprehensive collection of traditional Ecuadorian food recipes. It will include detailed cooking instructions, ingredient lists, and cultural background information about each dish. Additionally, it will feature user-submitted recipes and a membership form for a community of food enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the best recipes for traditional Ecuadorian desserts?
  • Scenario 2: Where can I find recipes that use native Ecuadorian ingredients?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #FF6347 (Tomato) - Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - Used for background colors and highlights.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto - Used for body text.

Secondary Font: Merriweather - Used for headings.
