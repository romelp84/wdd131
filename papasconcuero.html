Papas con Cuero Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 lb (450 g) pork belly
  • 2 red onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice potatoes into thin rounds. Set aside.
  2. Cut pork belly into small pieces. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat and fry pork belly until browned and crispy. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add sliced onions, tomatoes, minced garlic, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook until onions are soft.
  4. Add sliced potatoes to the pot and stir to combine with the onion mixture. Cook until potatoes are tender.
  5. Return the fried pork belly to the pot and mix well. Cook for another few minutes until heated through.
  6. Serve hot with rice or cornbread.
